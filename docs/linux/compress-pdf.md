### Compression presets

| Preset | Quality | Best for | File size reduction |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| /screen | Low (72 dpi) | Web, sharing, minimal size | Highest |
| /ebook | Medium (150 dpi) | **Recommended balance** | Good |
| /printer | High (300 dpi) | Printing | Moderate |
| /prepress | Very high (300 dpi) | Professional print | Lowest |
